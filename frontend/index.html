1 Smart Irrigation

AI-Optimised Water Scheduling

Enter your field conditions and location. NextGen Agri fetches the real 7-day weather forecast (Open-Meteo) and tells you exactly when to irrigate — saving 40–60% water vs flood methods.

How it works 1. Enter your location and field conditions

2. We fetch the real 7-day weather forecast from Open-Meteo for your exact coordinates

3. AI calculates irrigate / skip per day based on predicted rain & evapotranspiration

4. Water savings calculator shows your personal impact

3 Crop Recommendation

AI Crop Selection + Real Soil Data

Enter your location — NextGen Agri auto-fetches real soil properties from SoilGrids (ISRIC), live weather, and calamity news to recommend the best crops for this season.

Data Sources
🌡️ Live weather — Open-Meteo (free) 🌍 Soil data — SoilGrids ISRIC (global, free) 📰 Calamity news — NewsAPI 🤖 AI model — Random Forest Classifier
What you'll get
🌍 Real soil pH, nitrogen, texture from SoilGrids for your exact GPS coordinates 🌡️ Live temperature & humidity 📅 Season-aware recommendations (Kharif / Rabi / Zaid) ⚠️ Calamity-penalised scores — avoids flood-prone crops in drought year 📊 Top-5 crops with confidence scores and planting advisories
